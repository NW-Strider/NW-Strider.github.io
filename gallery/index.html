Entangled

A group collaboration By Jayde, Connor, Harley, and Nina for MAT 111FS

An effort to bridge the gap between the natural and unnatural through human connection, Entangled is a physical structure that employs wood, wire, 3D-printed items, as well as interactive lights and sounds. The exhibit was originally installed in the TRANslab of Elings Hall, but is in the process of moving to the Glassbox Gallery on campus. Our work addresses AI and equity by promoting inclusivity and accessibility. The interactive design ensures a wide audience can participate, showcasing AI as a tool that enhances human experiences without exclusion.
